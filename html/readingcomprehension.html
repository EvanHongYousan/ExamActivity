American school begins in September after a long summer holiday. There're two terms in a school year. The first term is from September to January, and the second term is from February to June. Most American children begin to go to school when they're five years old. Most students are seventeen or eighteen years old when they finish high school. High school students take only five or six subjects each term. They usually go to the same class every day, and they have homework for every class. After class, they do a lot of interesting things.

After high school, many students go to college(大学). They usually have to pay(付款)lots of money. So many college students work after class to get money for their studies.

1/ 2
1.

In America, summer holidays begin in ______.

  • AJuly
  • BSeptember
  • CMay
正确答案: A

方法分析

由“and the second term is from February to June”可知暑假由七月份开始。

2.

Most American children go to school at the age of ______.

  • Afive
  • Bseven
  • Ceighteen
正确答案: A

方法分析

由“ Most American children begin to go to school when they're five years old.”可知。

3.

High school students _____ after class.

  • Ado housework
  • Bgo to work
  • Cdo interesting things
正确答案: C

方法分析

由“After class, they do a lot of interesting things.”可知。

4.

In order to _____, many American college students work after class.

  • Ahelp their parents
  • Bget money for their studies
  • Chelp others
正确答案: B

方法分析

由“So many college students work after class to get money for their studies.”可知。

5.

After high school, many students go to _____.

  • Acities
  • Bwork
  • Ccollege
正确答案: C

方法分析

由“ After high school, many students go to college .”可知。
